TikTok’s Growing Popularity

By Nicole D'Costa

There is no doubt that since the pandemic started in March 2020, TikTok has become increasingly popular with people of all ages. TikTok is a popular video streaming and social media platform. It is one of the fastest growing social media platforms in the world.As of September 2021, TikTok has passed 1 billion monthly users after launching in August 2018.

TikTok became the most downloaded app in the world in the beginning of 2020 once the pandemic hit with 315 million downloads in that quarter alone. Since the launch in 2018, TikTok installs have soared to 3.2 billion.

ByteDance, TikTok’s parent company based in Beijing, saw its 2020 revenue more than double from 2019, to $34.3 billion. By June 2021, ByteDance was valued at $425 billion. Users can follow other accounts and also view content on the “For You” page which is generated by algorithms based on what the user interacts with. There is a wide variety of types of videos that users post on Tik Tok. From lip syncing videos, to tutorials, to comedic skits, there is a niche for everyone on the app. The videos can include different filters, music, and special effects to grab the audience's attention.

Hailey Bakos has a TikTok account with over six hundred thousand followers. “It was cool to see my videos and account gain attraction and attention as it showed people related to and enjoyed the content I was creating,” Bakos said.

With the growing success of TikTok, it was inevitable that other apps created video content of their own. Instagram launched a reels feature which is extremely similar to TikTok and Snapchat launched a spotlight feature that allows for video content from users. What differs TikTok from the rest of these video features on other social media platforms is the “For You” page. The app is very good at aligning with users’ personal interests to customize content that they would enjoy or relate to.

The growing popularity of the app can be attributed to numerous factors such as authenticity, a sense of community, unique content that one can’t find on another social media platform, and it is a place for people to express themselves as they please.

Users with large followings are even able to make money on the app. Some may even consider it their full time job. Addison Rae makes nearly $5 million per year on the app, making her the highest paid human on Tik Tok. She is followed by Charli D’Amelio who makes $4 million a year and her sister Dixie D’Amelio who makes $2.9 million a year.

Users can also profit off their videos from sponsored content such as paid collaborations or become part of TikTok’s Creator Fund. As part of the creator fund, creators can receive funds based on the number of views and the engagement on the content.

President Trump threatened to ban TikTok in the United States unless they were owned by an American company. TikTok then agreed to partner with Oracle and Walmart, but more recently President Biden has overturned Trump’s ban and will re-evaluate the risk the app poses to the country.
